Guide to Profile Encryption

Welcome to the Profile Encryption feature! This guide will help you understand what profile encryption is, why it’s important, and how to use it effectively.

What is Profile Encryption?

Profile encryption is a feature designed to protect the privacy and security of your personal information. By encrypting the name attached to your profile, you can hide your real name and other personal details from public view, replacing them with randomly generated names. This ensures that your identity remains confidential.

Why is Profile Encryption Important?

  • Privacy Protection: Safeguard your personal details from unauthorized access or exposure.
  • Security: Prevent potential threats by keeping your identity secure.
  • Control Over Personal Information: Decide who can see your real name and when.

How to Use Profile Encryption

Select a Reason for Encryption:

When you choose to encrypt your profile, you will be prompted to select a reason for doing so. This helps us understand and respect your privacy needs. The available options include privacy concerns, security purposes, avoiding unwanted attention, and more.

Encrypt Your Profile:

Once you’ve selected your reason, your profile’s name will be replaced with a randomly generated one. This encrypted name will be used wherever your profile appears on the platform.

Decryption:

If you ever wish to make your real name visible again, you can easily decrypt your profile. This will restore your original name across the platform.

By following these steps, you can ensure that your personal information remains secure and that you maintain control over your online identity.
