How to make a profile an External Person.

This guide explains how to change the role of a profile into an External Person. An External Person is part of a KNS group but not actively involved in group activities. They offer their skills and expertise to the community, making their profiles visible so others can leverage those skills.

Checking Eligibility

Ensure the person meets the requirements to become an External Person. The profile should be fully completed, including filling out their first name, last name, gender, date of birth, country, city, and role. Their email must be verified, and they must have agreed to the terms.

Eligibility Check: If the user does not meet the above requirements, the role change process will be halted, and an error message will be shown.

Informing the External Person

Once the user's role is successfully changed to External Person, a notification email is sent to the user. The email typically includes information on how their profile will be visible to other users in the application, so their skills and expertise can be leveraged for various initiatives.

Notification: It is important that the user is informed of their new role and how their profile will be used within the application, as their expertise can now be accessed by other users.
