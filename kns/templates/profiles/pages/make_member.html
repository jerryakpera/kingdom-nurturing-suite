How to make a profile a member role.

This guide explains how to change the role of a profile into a member. Once the user becomes a member, their group leader will manage their profile until they can gain access to the application by forming their own DBS group.

Checking Eligibility

Make sure the person you're promoting meets the requirements to become a member. The person should have a fully completed profile, which includes filling out their first name, last name, gender, date of birth, country, city, and role. Their email must be verified, and they must have agreed to the terms.

Eligibility Check: If the user does not meet the above requirements, the role change process will be halted, and an error message will be shown.

Informing the new member

Once the user's role is successfully changed to member, a notification email is sent to the user. The email typically includes instructions on how they can access their profile once they become a leader.

Notification: It is important that the user is informed of their new role and that they know the steps required to gain full access to their profile by forming their own group.
