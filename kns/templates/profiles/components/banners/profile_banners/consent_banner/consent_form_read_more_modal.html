Consent form information

To comply with legal requirements, users under the required adult age must submit a consent form from a parent or legal guardian. If you are an underage user, or if you manage underage members in your group, please review the following details about the consent form process:

Consent Form Submission:
- Who Submits: The consent form must be completed and submitted by the parent or legal guardian of the underage user.
- How to Submit: Download the consent form by clicking the Download Consent Form button in the profiles page, have it signed by a guardian, and then visit the user's profile to upload the signed form.

Status Tracking:
- Monitoring Status: Track the status of the consent form within the application. It will show as "Pending" until reviewed by an admin.
- Approval Process: The form will be marked as "Approved" if accepted, or "Rejected" if there are issues. Make sure to follow up to ensure all necessary consents are in place.

Review and Approval Process:
- Admin Review: Admins or designated reviewers will review the form. Check the status and review details on the user's profile page.
- Rejection Handling: If rejected, a reason will be provided. Address any issues and resubmit a corrected form as needed.

By ensuring the consent form is properly submitted and approved, you can continue to use the system's full features without any restrictions.
