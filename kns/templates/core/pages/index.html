Discipleship with Innovation

The Kingdom Nurturing Suite is designed to empower Disciple Making Movements by providing a robust platform for managing relationships, tracking progress, and sharing experiences. It aims to facilitate the holistic growth of individuals and groups/teams, enabling them to fulfill their mission effectively and impactfully

KNS is designed to aid discipleship efforts while maintaining simplicity and ease of use.

Empowering Disciple Making Movements

Kingdom Nurturing Suite (KNS) is designed to support the integrated Disciple Making Movement by facilitating the effective administration, management, and growth tracking of Discovery Bible Study (DBS) groups and their activities.

Track & Share Your Discipleship Journey

Administer, coordinate, and report on DMM activities with KNS. Track growth, register groups, and facilitate equipping processes while sharing and learning from others' experiences.

Register and Track Group Multiplication

Create and manage DBS groups online, track member transformations, and support the development of emerging leaders who can form their own groups for generational multiplication.

Mutually Equip and Grow with other Disciples

Network and collaborate with peers, find mentors and mentees, and utilize diverse practices to enhance your movement experience and maximize the five-fold ministries.
